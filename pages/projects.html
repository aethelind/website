Shuttle Tracker

The uOttawa Shuttle is free to ride, and moves hundreds of students between uO campuses every day. For my honours project, I created proof-of-concept for a cheap, reliable, scalable tracking system for the shuttle. The system consists of two cross-platform mobile apps and a real-time database to connect them. One app stays with the driver and reports the shuttle's location and projected arrival times to the database. The other app presents this location data to the commuter. The apps share data by connecting to a Firebase. Both apps were built in Ionic.
